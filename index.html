About Us

An Nam Phở is a family-owned Vietnamese restaurant located in Seattle near the Roosevelt Sound Transit Light Rail station. We are dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Vietnam to your table. Our specialty is Phở – a fragrant and flavorful rice noodle soup that has been a staple in Vietnamese cuisine for generations.

At An Nam Phở, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect with loved ones and create lasting memories. Our recipes have been passed down through our family, each bowl of Phở crafted with love, tradition, and the freshest ingredients. From our rich, slow-cooked broth to our tender slices of beef and fresh herbs, every element is carefully prepared to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.
